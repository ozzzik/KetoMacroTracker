About the app

Keto Macro Tracker helps you track your macros and stay on track with your ketogenic diet. The app is free and supported by ads; you can optionally watch an ad in Profile for an ad-free experience until the end of the day.

For Developers

Requirements: iOS 15.0+, Xcode 14.0+, Swift 5.7+

Setup: Clone the repository, open in Xcode, and build. See the README for details.
