### 4. Support URL (Required)
**Purpose**: Where users can get help with your app.

**Options**:
- **If you have a website**: `https://yourwebsite.com/support`
- **If you have a help page**: `https://yourwebsite.com/help`
- **If using email**: `mailto:support@yourwebsite.com`
- **If you have a contact form**: `https://yourwebsite.com/contact`

**Example**:
```
https://ketomacrotracker.com/support
```

**Note**: Replace `http://example.com` with your actual support URL.

---

### 5. Marketing URL (Optional)
**Purpose**: Your app's marketing website.

**Options**:
- **If you have a website**: `https://yourwebsite.com`
- **If you have a landing page**: `https://yourwebsite.com/app`
- **Leave empty if you don't have one**

**Example**:
```
https://ketomacrotracker.com
```

**Note**: This is optional - you can leave it empty or remove the example URL.

---

### 6. Version
**Purpose**: The version number of your app.

**Default**: `1.0`

**Keep as**: `1.0` (or match your app's version in Xcode)

**Note**: This should match the version in your Xcode project's `Info.plist` or project settings.

---

**For Your App**: **Leave this empty** - Your app is a health/fitness tracker, not a routing app.

## Tips

1. **Promotional Text**: Update this frequently to highlight new features or promotions
2. **Description**: Use bullet points and emojis to make it scannable
3. **Keywords**: Focus on terms users actually search for
4. **Support URL**: Make sure it's accessible and helpful
5. **Copyright**: Use your legal name or registered company name
