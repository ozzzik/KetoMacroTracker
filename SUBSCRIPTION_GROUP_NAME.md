# Subscription Group Display Name

## What to Fill In

In the "Add App Store Localization" dialog, fill in:

### Subscription Group Display Name
**Enter**: `Premium Subscription`

**OR** you can use:
- `Keto Macro Tracker Premium`
- `Premium Features`
- `Premium`

**Recommended**: `Premium Subscription` (matches your code and is clear to users)

## Character Limit
- Maximum: 75 characters
- Keep it short and clear

## What This Does
This name appears to users when they manage their subscriptions in Settings → App Store → Subscriptions. It groups your monthly and yearly subscriptions together.

## Complete the Form

1. **Localization**: Already selected - "English (U.S.)" ✅
2. **Subscription Group Display Name**: Enter `Premium Subscription`
3. **App Name Display Options**: Keep "Use App Name KetoMacroTracker" selected ✅
4. Click **"Create"**

After filling in "Premium Subscription", the "Create" button should become enabled.
