# App Store Screenshots Guide

## Required Screenshot Dimensions

You need screenshots in these dimensions:
- **1242 × 2688px** (Portrait) or **2688 × 1242px** (Landscape)
- **1284 × 2778px** (Portrait) or **2778 × 1284px** (Landscape)

## Which Simulator to Use

### Option 1: iPhone 14 Pro Max (Recommended)
- **Dimensions**: 1284 × 2778px (Portrait) / 2778 × 1284px (Landscape)
- **Why**: Most modern, matches latest iPhone sizes
- **In Xcode**: Product → Destination → iPhone 14 Pro Max

### Option 2: iPhone 13 Pro Max
- **Dimensions**: 1284 × 2778px (Portrait) / 2778 × 1284px (Landscape)
- **Why**: Same dimensions as iPhone 14 Pro Max
- **In Xcode**: Product → Destination → iPhone 13 Pro Max

### Option 3: iPhone 11 Pro Max / iPhone XS Max
- **Dimensions**: 1242 × 2688px (Portrait) / 2688 × 1242px (Landscape)
- **Why**: Older but still valid dimensions
- **In Xcode**: Product → Destination → iPhone 11 Pro Max

## Recommended: iPhone 14 Pro Max

**Use iPhone 14 Pro Max** because:
- ✅ Matches the larger dimensions (1284 × 2778px)
- ✅ Most current iPhone model
- ✅ Better for showcasing your app
- ✅ Apple prefers latest device screenshots

## How to Take Screenshots

### Step 1: Choose Simulator
1. In Xcode: **Product → Destination → iPhone 14 Pro Max**
2. Or: **Window → Devices and Simulators** → Select iPhone 14 Pro Max

### Step 2: Run Your App
1. **Product → Run** (Cmd+R)
2. Navigate to the screens you want to capture

### Step 3: Take Screenshots
1. **Device → Screenshot** (Cmd+S) in Simulator menu
2. OR: **File → New Screen Recording** then take frame
3. Screenshots are saved to Desktop by default

### Step 4: Verify Dimensions
- Screenshots should be exactly:
  - **1284 × 2778px** (Portrait)
  - **2778 × 1284px** (Landscape)

## Screenshots You Need

For App Store Connect, you typically need:

### Required:
- **6.7" Display** (iPhone 14 Pro Max / 13 Pro Max):
  - At least 1 portrait screenshot (1284 × 2778px)
  - Optional: Landscape (2778 × 1284px)

### Optional (but recommended):
- **6.5" Display** (iPhone 11 Pro Max):
  - Portrait (1242 × 2688px)
  - Landscape (2688 × 1242px)

## Quick Steps

1. **Open Xcode**
2. **Select Simulator**: iPhone 14 Pro Max
3. **Run your app**: Cmd+R
4. **Navigate to key screens**:
   - Dashboard/Home screen
   - Food search
   - Food log
   - Profile/Settings
5. **Take screenshot**: Cmd+S (or Device → Screenshot)
6. **Screenshots saved to Desktop**

## Tips

- **Show key features**: Dashboard, food logging, analytics
- **Use real data**: Make it look like a real user experience
- **Clean UI**: Hide any debug elements
- **Multiple screens**: Show different features
- **Portrait preferred**: Most users view in portrait

## After Taking Screenshots

1. **Verify dimensions**: Check file properties
2. **Crop if needed**: Should be exact dimensions
3. **Upload to App Store Connect**: 
   - App Store Connect → Your App → Version → Screenshots
   - Drag and drop your screenshots
